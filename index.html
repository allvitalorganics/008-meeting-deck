A — The Vision
3 / 30

What 008 Is

An application-only curated social evening held in intimate venues.

  • A room where every guest has been reviewed and approved before entry
  • A recurring night with a new theme each time, teased but never fully revealed
  • An experience designed by a host who reads the room, not a promoter who fills it
  • A physical invitation you keep on your desk. A night you think about afterward.

What 008 Is Not

Not a dating app. Not speed dating. Not a matchmaking service.

Not a nightclub. Not a mixer. Not a brand activation.

Not networking. Not a startup event. Not a happy hour.

Not ethnicity-targeted. Not age-gated beyond 21+. Not a niche play.

The word "dating" never appears on any public material. 008 promises one thing: the room will be worth being in.

The Name

"Two zeros. An eight. You'll figure it out."

B — The Brand
10 / 30

Photography Direction

No faces. Just energy. Atmospheric.

Shoot

Candid over posed. Low light over flash. The room empty and full. Drinks in hand. Laughter. The host at work. Details: cards, signage, candles. Warm shadows. Green undertones in post. Grain is acceptable.

Never

Flash photography. Posed group shots. Selfies. Branding overlays on photos. Wide-angle venue listing shots. Anyone who has not consented. Bright or overlit images. Filters.

Post-Production

  • Desaturated. Pull back color intensity 20-30%.
  • Warm shadows — push toward amber/green, never blue.
  • Contrast: moderate. Never crushed blacks, never blown highlights.
  • Skin tones stay natural. Desaturate the environment, not the people.

Privacy is paramount. The photography should feel like a memory. Something you walked into, not something staged for you. No identifiable faces in public-facing content without explicit consent.
