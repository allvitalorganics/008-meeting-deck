The Surgeon General declared loneliness a public health crisis. Bumble went from $70 to $3.40. Match Group from $170 to $31. Both are laying off up to 30% of staff. The app era is ending. The IRL era is beginning.

50% of US adults experience loneliness. People want to meet people. They just do not have a place that respects the act of trying.

Nobody owns "premium IRL dating." No branded, scaled, multi-city premium dating event company exists. The space is fragmented across local speed dating operators. 008 is first-mover in a category that is about to explode.

03

What 008 Is

An application-only curated social evening held in intimate venues.

  • A room where every guest has been reviewed and approved before entry
  • A recurring night with a new theme each time, teased but never fully revealed
  • An experience designed by a host who reads the room, not a promoter who fills it
  • A physical invitation you keep on your desk. A night you think about afterward.

What 008 Is Not

Not a dating app. Not speed dating. Not a matchmaking service.

Not a nightclub. Not a mixer. Not a brand activation.

Not networking. Not a startup event. Not a happy hour.

Not ethnicity-targeted. Not age-gated beyond 21+. Not a niche play.

The word "dating" never appears on any public material. 008 promises one thing: the room will be worth being in. The people will be worth meeting. The night will be worth remembering.
